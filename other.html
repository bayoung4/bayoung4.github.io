Woodworking FAQ

Essential Woodworking Tools

Chisel
- Used to remove material in small amounts with precision.
Square
- Used to square wood.
Hammers
- Used to pound chisels, join wood, and sink nails.
No. 4 Hand Plane (Link to my recommended starter plane)
- Used to flatten wood.
Spokeshave
- Used to shave off material on contoured wood.
Files and Rasps
- Used to roughly remove material.
Saws
- Used for cutting with or against the grain.
Drills
- Used for drilling holes of various sizes.
Combination Guage
- Used for marking straight lines.
Sliding Bevel Guide
- Used for marking dovetail joinery.
